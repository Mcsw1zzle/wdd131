Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to create a safe and reliable tool that can be used to discover new adventures. This website will show a list of trails with all of their detailed information, such as terrain and difficulty. You will be able to search for trails based off of their information as well.

Audience

The target audience is anyone out there who loves the outdoors, regardless of age or ability. Anyone who wants to embark on a new adventure will benefit from this website.

Dynamic elements

Dynamic elements will include a useable searchbar, as well as the ability to report any issues with a specific trail. It will also have the ability to rate their experience with the trail.
